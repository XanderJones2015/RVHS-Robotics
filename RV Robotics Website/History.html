The History of the RVHS Robotics Team

BEST Robotics was brought to RVHS in 2015 when previous Manning team members Holly Vose and Aidan Gould decided to start a team there. As a rookie team we did fantastic and immediately gained recognition by Rocky Mountain BEST as a team that was there to win. We hope that once the final founding members graduate in the class of 2019 the team will continue to carry on the legacy at Ralston Valley and continue keeping this log updated.
